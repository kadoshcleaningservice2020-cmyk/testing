Carpet Cleaning

Hot Water Extraction · All Fibre Types
What's Included

Professional Carpet Care

Carpets harbour allergens, bacteria, pet dander, and deep-set stains that regular vacuuming simply cannot remove. Our truck-mounted hot water extraction method cleans deep into the fibres — removing contaminants and refreshing the look, feel, and smell of your carpets.

  • Pre-Inspection: We assess each carpet's fibre type and condition to select the ideal cleaning method.

  • Pre-Treatment: Problem areas and stains pre-sprayed with targeted cleaning solutions.

  • Hot Water Extraction: Truck-mounted steam cleaning penetrates deep into carpet fibres to remove dirt and bacteria.

  • Stain Removal: Coffee, wine, pet stains, and more — we use specialist treatments for stubborn spots.

  • Deodorising: Neutralises odours from pets, smoke, and mildew at the source.

  • Fast Drying: Professional air movers used to speed up drying time — typically 2–4 hours.
